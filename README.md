# Stremini Workspace

Stremini Workspace is a premium, AI-powered productivity suite designed to revolutionize how you interact with Google Workspace. It leverages Gemini's intelligence to generate, edit, and manage professional documents, forms, and presentations through natural language.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📝 AI Google Forms
- **One-Prompt Generation**: Generate complex job applications, customer surveys, or event registrations in seconds.
- **Interactive Builder**: Drag-and-drop questions to reorder, edit titles/types, and refine your form.
- **AI Editing**: Need a change? Just ask the AI to "add a question about experience" or "make this more professional."
- **Live Preview**: See your form exactly as respondents will see it before publishing.
- **Google Integration**: Publish directly to your Google account or edit existing forms from your Drive.

### 📊 AI Google Slides
- **Deck Orchestration**: Transform a simple topic into a multi-slide narrative deck with coordinated headers and robust, detailed body text.
- **Intelligent Layouts**: Specialized layouts like "Market Analysis" designed for data-heavy strategic insights.
- **Visual Flexibility**: Support for manual image uploads and AI-suggested image prompts for every slide.
- **AI Insight Panel**: 
  - **Summarization**: Instantly summarize entire decks for quick review.
  - **Q&A**: Ask deep conversational questions about your presentation content to uncover hidden insights.
- **Interactive Preview**: High-fidelity in-app preview mode with theme-aligned typography and improved text visibility.
- **Batch Publishing**: High-performance sync creates the full presentation structure, ensuring professional alignment and font consistency.

### 📂 Google Drive Management
- **Unified Browser**: View and search your most recent Google Workspace files.
- **Smart Sorting**: Filter and sort by name, creation date, or modification time.
- **Direct Actions**: Open files directly in Google Workspace or manage them via the cloud.

### 🎨 Premium Design & Experience
- **Professional UI**: A polished, modern interface built with Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion for smooth transitions.
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully adaptive design for desktops, tablets, and phones.
- **Secure OAuth**: Integrated Google Authentication ensures your data remains private and secure.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 18, Vite, TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Animations**: Motion (framer-motion)
- **AI**: Gemini 1.5 & Gemini 2.0 (Google Generative AI SDK)
- **Integrations**: Google Workspace APIs (Drive, Forms, Slides), Google OAuth 2.0

## 🚦 Getting Started
1. **Sign In**: Click the "Sign in with Google" button to grant permissions for Drive, Forms, and Slides.
2. **Select Tool**: Use the sidebar to navigate between Drive, Forms, and Slides.
3. **Generate**: Enter a prompt in the AI Builder (e.g., "Create a feedback form for my restaurant") and watch the AI work.
4. **Publish**: Once satisfied, click "Publish to Google" to save the file to your Drive.
